Instructions: You must grow enough predator red cells to use in a medicine. They only eat the herbivore grey cells. Use the tools at the bottom to maniuplate the simulation and let all the cells thrive - but using them isn't free! Extract larger predators to earn money.
Tools are as follows from left to right:
Place plant cell
Move cell from one location to another
Instantly split cell
Irradiate cell to make it likely to gain a mutation on the next reproduction
Extract cell for money.
